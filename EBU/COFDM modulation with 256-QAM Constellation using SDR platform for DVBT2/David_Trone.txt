H.J.Res. 98, Resolution of Disapproval on Joint Employer Rule
Working people opposed this resolution that would overturn the National Labor Relations Board’s pro-worker joint employer rule, which ensured that all companies that control working conditions in a workplace are at the table for good-faith bargaining.

S.J.Res. 38, Dealing with Buy America Requirements
Working people opposed this resolution that favored foreign producers and materials while sidelining American workers by undermining Buy America requirements for electric vehicle infrastructure development.
