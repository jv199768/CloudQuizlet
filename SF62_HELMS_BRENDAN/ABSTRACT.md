DOT is continuing to work with Loudoun County to move forward with the following improvements along Route 50 (Little River Turnpike):

Transforming the westbound inner left-turn lane at the Route 50 and Pleasant Valley Road (Loudoun) intersection into an auxiliary through lane extending to the inner westbound left-turn lane at Tall Cedars Parkway.
Extending the existing eastbound left-turn lane to Arcola Boulevard and adding a second left turn lane. 
Within the project limits, Route 50 average traffic counts range from 18,000 to 48,000 vehicles a day.

The project aims to improve safety and operations.
